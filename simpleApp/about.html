About National Youth Corps

Established in 2003, the National Youth Corps has been at the forefront of youth development in Sri Lanka, offering comprehensive training programs and opportunities for personal growth.

Our Mission

“To produce knowledgeable, competent, skillful, disciplined and energetic youth enriched with resilience to challenges through life-skill development and to contribute towards the youth development in Sri Lanka by filling the skill gap necessary for the vocational professionalism.”

Our VISION

“To be the leading state organization in the Asian region for life skill development of the youth.”

Discipline

Fostering self-control, time management, and commitment through structured training programs that instill valuable life principles and work ethics.

Personality

Building strong character traits, emotional intelligence, and interpersonal skills through engaging activities and workshops that shape well-rounded individuals.

Leadership

Developing future leaders through hands-on experience, mentorship programs, and strategic thinking exercises that enhance decision-making and team management skills.
