Spearhead Your Future with Us

The Youth Corps established under the Parliament Act No. 21 of 2002 commenced its operations through Youth Corps Regulation No. 01-2003 of the Gazette Notification No.1309/17, 10th October 2003. As a public institute under the Ministry of Policy Development and Implementation, we have been empowering young individuals for over two decades, providing essential skills, resources, and opportunities for personal and professional growth.
